ASHLEY MAGANA

Physical Manifestations of Myself. I am currently attending San Jose State University in pursuit of a higher education in the Art of Ceramics. My love for ceramics derives from me younger years playing in the mud and has flourished into a fulltime education. I also use photography as a medium to convey the love and confidence I have for myself. Creating art with different mediums has been a life long pursuit to find what truly calls to me as an artist. I am constantly trying to perfect and concentrate my art into the purest form of self love and physical manifestations of Myself
